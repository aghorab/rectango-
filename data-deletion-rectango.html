About data in Rectango

Rectango does not require an account and does not store your name, email address, or contacts. Your game progress (such as your current level) is stored only on your own device and is removed when you uninstall the app. The only data processed off your device is advertising data handled by Google AdMob (such as your device advertising identifier and app-interaction data) and, if you make a purchase, purchase information handled by Google Play.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to aghorab.slovakia@gmail.com with the subject Rectango — Data Deletion Request.
  2. In the message, state that you are requesting deletion of your data and tell us the approximate date you last used the app (this helps us locate any related advertising records).
  3. We will acknowledge your request and process it within 30 days.

You can also reset or delete your advertising identifier yourself at any time in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Reset/Delete advertising ID), which stops further association of ad data with your device.

Retention

We do not operate our own servers and do not retain personal data beyond what the services above process. Advertising data is governed by Google's retention policies: policies.google.com/privacy. Purchase records may be retained by Google Play for the period required by applicable Slovak and EU tax law.
